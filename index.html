About Me

Educator. Designer.
Quality Guardian.

I'm a Mathematics specialist and educational content professional with 9+ years of experience spanning classroom teaching, digital content design, print quality control, and social impact program management.

I started as a Math teacher — but what drove me was always the question: is this content actually working for the learner? That curiosity led me into curriculum design at Egyptian Japanese Schools, where I built interactive digital lessons for the WinjiGo platform and collaborated with Japanese educational experts. Then into quality control at Nahdet Misr, where I led the scientific review of the Al-Adwaa series and oversaw educational products for four international markets.

In parallel, I founded KADA — a teacher professional development program focused on mental health awareness — which won the Nitrous Mega Competition 2022 and was presented at Giza Systems Foundation's Social Impact Acceleration Program.

Today, I'm deepening my expertise in Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) and actively pursuing roles where I can lead the full content lifecycle — from design to impact measurement.

KADA — Mental Health Awareness for Teachers

KADA is a structured teacher professional development program I designed from the ground up — addressing burnout, anxiety, and stress among educators, and equipping them with practical resilience and coping strategies. The program combines facilitated sessions, interactive tools like the "Mental Health Wheel," bilingual workbooks, and a peer support network.

I managed the full project lifecycle: from strategic vision and budget management, to partnership development with NGOs and educational institutions, to facilitating workshops and tracking program outcomes through an M&E framework.
